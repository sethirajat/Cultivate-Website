About Cultivate

Located on the street level of Elm Hall, Cultivate is a special destination for students, faculty, staff and guests who want to experience full-service dining with a nod to locally sourced fresh food and a community feel. The menu celebrates new discoveries and memories of food, and features a Pacific Northwest twist on regional comforts. Try our savory signature house-smoked pastrami or shrimp po'boy sandwich. Vegetarians can revel in the fresh, crisp tastes of our seasonal market salad, veggie quesadillas or the mac bar.


John Vandeley

John is our dedicated Head Chef. He has more than 8 years of culinary experience, having led numerous restaurants across the globe. In his most recent gig, John managed the kitchen for Hyatt Park Bangkok, a five-star luxury restaurant well known for its fine Eurasian dishes. Now living in Seattle, John sees this as an exciting opportunity to truly work with locally sourced fresh food - his lifelong passion. With his knowledge of international cuisines, he's excited to bring to Cultivate dishes you will love to eat and tell others about.


Kenny Lee

As the Lead Manager, Kenny brings to Cultivate 16 years of culinary management experience. Besides being a food lover, Kenny's management philosophy starts with the client. The client is always right, and the existence of Cultivate relies on the full satisfaction of its clients. As a manager, Kenny also tries to build a strong bond with his staff. Now in his fifth year at Cultivate, the restaurant has become a second home to Kenny. He welcomes you to come and try out our diverse menu, with new and exciting dishes being introduced everyday.

Our Specialties

Our menu changes with the seasons, and you'll find exciting new dishes to try that are featured on the daily fresh sheet everyday. Our ingredients are all locally influenced, seasonally motivated, and sustainably minded. Check out some of the specialty dishes that have helped made Cultivate a popular choice for students, faculty, and staff at the University of Washington - as well as elsewhere!

STARTER

Ahi Bites

A fresh sheet special—cucumber, grilled rare ahi tuna, citrus wasabi aioli and candied citrus zest. A beautiful combination of the Pacific Northwest taste and the tropical fruits, giving you and long-lasting impression which you'll never forget.

Onion Rings

Our thick-cut onion rings are made from whole white onions, battered with a subtle blend of spices, letting the onion’s natural sweetness shine through. Fried golden after you order and salted the second they leave the fryer - a perfect way to start.

MAIN

The Burger

One of the "Best Burgers Around Puget Sound" according to KING 5 viewers. A 6 oz hand-hewn patty, house slather, Peppashire pub cheese, pickles, lettuce, tomato and red onion on a butter-toasted pretzel roll. One word — incredible.

Steak Sandwich

Tender, flavorful and perfectly seasoned prime rib, sliced thin, topped with smoked gouda cheese and creamy horseradish sauce and served on a ciabatta bun. Served with a side of au jus - never a better dish to enjoy your summer days.

DESSERT

Coffee Cheesecake

The scrumptious cheesecake, baked in a chocolate cookie crust, flavored with coffee and drizzled with melted chocolate and a coffee-caramel sauce. The true Seattleite dessert, incorporating our city's icon into your sweet desires.

Vanilla Mousse

This light, creamy vanilla dessert is heaven in a teaspoon. The ultimate compliment to your heavy main course, with a strawberry layer on top to give you that fresh taste along with the delightful sweetness. Unsurprisingly, one of our top orders.
